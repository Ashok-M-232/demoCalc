1Ô∏è‚É£ Role of SOC L3

SOC L3 analysts or Incident Responders are senior experts responsible for leading advanced investigations, incident management, malware reverse engineering, threat intelligence, and mentoring SOC L1 & L2 teams.

Primary Responsibilities

  • Lead and manage complex security incidents
  • Perform malware reverse engineering and advanced endpoint analysis
  • Integrate threat intelligence to detect targeted attacks
  • Guide SOC L1/L2 and improve detection systems
  • Coordinate IR with internal teams and external partners
  • Develop and refine incident response playbooks
  • Provide executive-level incident reports and strategic recommendations

SOC L3 Mindset

  • Expert in cyber threat analysis
  • Strategic, calm, and decisive under pressure
  • Evidence-driven and technically precise
  • Proactive attacker-centric thinking
  • Deep understanding of defensive architecture and controls

  • Refine SIEM/SOAR rules and use cases
  • Develop new playbooks and automate repetitive tasks
  • Prepare detailed reports for management and stakeholders
