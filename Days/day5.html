SOC L2 analysts are senior operators responsible for validating complex alerts, deep investigation, incident response, threat hunting, malware analysis, tuning detection systems, and mentoring SOC L1.

Primary Responsibilities

  • Validate and confirm real security incidents
  • Perform root cause analysis and incident triage
  • Guide SOC L1 and review escalations
  • Handle medium to high severity incidents
  • Collaborate with IR, Forensics, and Threat Intel teams
  • Improve SIEM rules, use cases, detections
  • Prepare incident reports and executive summaries

SOC L2 Mindset

  • Analytical and calm during incidents
  • Evidence-based investigation
  • Attack-oriented thinking
  • Defensive architecture understanding

  • Tune SIEM to reduce false positives
  • Work on playbooks and automation
  • Reporting and documentation

4Ô∏è‚É£ Incident Handling and Response (SOC L2)

Containment Techniques

  • Disable compromised accounts
  • Isolate host from network
  • Block malicious IP/domain
  • Stop malicious processes
